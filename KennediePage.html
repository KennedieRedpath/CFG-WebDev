Alongside my day job, I’m also the owner of Hinterland Hedgehogs, where I aim to promote the ethical keeping and breeding of African Pygmy Hedgehogs. Since 2020, I’ve worked to build and promote my brand via social media, connecting with owners across the UK, and even other parts of the world!

As well as running a pedigreed breeding programme, I foster and rehabilitate APH in need, and I’m an admin for the African Pygmy Hedgehog Club UK, providing support and guidance to 16,000 members across the UK.

African Pygmys are an exotic species that require a lot of specialist care, but they can also be amazingly rewarding pets and wonderful companions!
